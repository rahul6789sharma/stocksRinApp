Buying Call ﻿Options﻿ (Long Call Option) Explained

Buying calls (sometimes referred to as being "long calls") is an option strategy that consists of buying a call option on a stock that a trader believes will increase in price

When to Use this Strategy The strategy comes handy when you have a bullish view on the stock/index.

Trade Setup
Buy 1 Strike Call Option

Important Points about Strategy

  1. This is 1 leg Strategy
  2. This is net debit strategy
  3. This strategy has limited risk and unlimited profit potential

E.g Nifty

Call Strike (ITM, Long) 8600
Lower Strike Premium Paid 150
  • Net Debit : Premium Paid for Call strike = 150 (per lot)
  • Max Profit Potential : Unlimited
  • Max Loss Potential : Net Debit 150 (per lot)
  • BreakEven Point : Call Strike + Net Debit = 8600 + 150 = 8750
